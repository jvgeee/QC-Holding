What is coworking?

Coworking to us is that feeling of being more productive when being in the presence of others. It’s a style of work that involves a shared work environment, but with independent activity. Coworking can take place anywhere, but we choose to do it at Collins Collective.

What is the best way to see the space?

Drop in! It’s best to let us know before you decide to come in so we can ensure there’s someone from the core team there who can show you around.

Can I use Collins Collective as my business address?

Resident and Partner members may use the space as their business address provided they pick up their mail often.

How do I get to Collins Collective?

We’re at the top-end of Collins street, near Spring street. Across the road is the Sofitel hotel.

The Spring street entrance to Parliament station is 3 minute walk away. Collins street is also serviced by tram routes 109, 112, 48. The stop is right outside the building.

Can I host an event at Collins Collective?

We love events at Collins Collective, especially if they’re of benefit to our members and the coworking scene in general. Send us an email with details on your event and we’ll try make things happen.
